Our Mission

RICA Commercial's mission is to encourage the world to raise their standards of reduction, recycling, and reuse through providing economic and efficient ways for individuals and companies to safely and responsibly recycle electronics. We aim to dynamically change the way we recycle eletronic waste in the United States in a sustainable and economical way.

With over 20 years of experience in e-waste management, RICA Commercial gaurantees a quality recycling experience. Our environmentally sound process and advanced technologies enable us to handle and service any company of any size. We provide a free local drop off service to any California residence at our primary location in Hayward, Ca.

RICA Commercial has a simple paperless process for e-waste collection. As your recycling partner, we will guide you through the entire process, while guaranteeing that all of our electronic waste will be recycled within the United States following strict health and environmental regulations.
